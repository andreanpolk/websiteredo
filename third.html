Current Exhibition: The Eighth Sense
A cleverly coded couture piece, fashioned from materials responsive to real-time digital media, which change its colour remotely, bringing the digital to the physical.

The Unseen aims to discover and investigate the human state by using a physical garment that reads human magnetism, translated through the sculpture by the use of colour and pattern.

For instance red portrays anger, nerves and anxiety, whereas green reflects teaching, sociality and people. Blue reveals calming, truthfulness and peace while white mirrors an inner state of sensitivity, intuitiveness and psychic ability.

Make a private booking to see your own Aura. What will you see?

Past Exhibition: Beauty – Cooper Hewitt Design Triennial
The Scarab, part of the A I R collection has now launched at the Cooper Hewitt in New York for the “Beauty-Triennial” exhibition.

“Beauty” celebrates design as creative endeavour that engages with the mind, body and senses. With a focus on aesthetic, innovation, the exhibition will feature more than 250 works by 63 designers from around the globe.
